<h3>Description</h3>
<p>An EhViewer fork with classic Material Design 2 style</p>
<p>This fork is for personal use and does not accept feature requests; for common usage issues, please refer to the <a href="https://github.com/EhViewer-NekoInverter/EhViewer/issues/18">Q&amp;A</a></p>
<p>If you prefer Material Design 3, consider using <a href="https://github.com/FooIbar/EhViewer">EhViewer-Overhauled</a></p>
